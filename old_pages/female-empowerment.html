Gender inequality is a real thing...

Unfortunately that is still not clear to people. There are 190 heads of state - only 9 of them are women. Off all the people in parliament in the world, 13% are women. We believe that technology can change that. This is why we have a particular focus on empowering girls by teaching them to work with technology at their early ages.

Teachers as role models

Over 70% of teachers in German schools are women. We believe those teachers can serve as role models.

Equality starts in schools

Bringing technology into the classrooms will enable boys & girls equally to engage with technology.
